Lottery History Rules

Rules of the game:

● The game is a blockachain based Lottery, Launched on Goerly testnet.

● Each bet costs 0.01 Goerly ETH. Make sure you have enough ETH!

● To play this game, first you should connect your wallet using metamask.

● After connection of the wallet you should select 5 numbers from 1 to 99.

● Numbers should not repeat!

● When you press play button, the game randomly sets 5 numbers.

● The game also chooses numbers that do not repeat.

● Then your numbers are compared to numbers that were set by hte game:

▶ If you get 1 match, you will get half of the initial price.

▶ If you get 2 matches, you will get amount egual to the initial price.

▶ If you get 3 matches, you will get doubled amount of initial price.

▶ If you get 4 matches, you will get 0.5 Goerly ETH.

▶ If you get 5 matches, you will get 1 Goerly ETH.

● You can get your rewards in history tab.

● You can get rewards after each play or all at once, it is up to you.
